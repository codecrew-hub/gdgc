Understanding Verb Forms & Tenses

Verb Forms

Verbs can take different forms depending on tense and subject. The main forms are:

  • Base Form: e.g., play, go, eat
  • Past Simple: e.g., played, went, ate
  • Past Participle: e.g., played, gone, eaten
  • Present Participle: e.g., playing, going, eating
